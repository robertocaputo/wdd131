Blue

Blue — This name reflects the mission of connecting real estate property owners and licensed agents, similar to how Uber connects passengers and drivers.

Optional domain availability: blue.com.br

Site Purpose

The purpose of Blue is to simplify and modernize the real estate market. The platform allows property owners to pay a small fee to advertise their property and automatically connect with nearby real estate agents. Agents receive alerts based on location and property type, and users can communicate, schedule visits, and complete documentation through the platform.

Scenarios

  • How can I quickly advertise my house and get in touch with a real estate agent in my area?
  • What is the cost for agents to receive property alerts and how do I subscribe as a realtor?

Color Scheme

  • #FBBF24 (Warm Yellow) – Used for accent elements, call-to-action highlights, and icons.

Typography
